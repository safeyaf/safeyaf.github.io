     478 ° Theatre

The corporate identity design for 478 Degrees, an experimental theatre in Berlin, captures the essence of the theatre's focus on innovative performances that intentionally break traditional conventions and challenge the norm.

The identity reflects the broad scope of exploration and the pursuit of different angles and new perspectives, thus the uncoventional name “ 478 Degrees ”.

The design includes the creation of a logo, letterheads, business cards and three posters, all of which play with the concept of degrees.

Corporate identity design. Muthesius University | 2024
