Cairo Opera House:
National graphic art exhibition

Poster, book and branding for The Fourth National Graphic Art Exhibition. The exhibition invited graphic artists, involved in printmaking; such as linoleum and wood-block printing, etching and silkscreen printing. The exhibition displayed; artworks from renowned national Egyptian artists, as well as new young artists. It took place at the Palace of Art, at the Cairo Opera House (January 2016). The poster later won at the 100 Best Arabic Posters competition.

Supervised by Haytham Nawwar
Cairo | 2016
