   Table Tales
        The Global
  Nomad Cuisine
          of Abu Dhabi

“Table Tales: The Global Nomad Cuisine of Abu Dhabi” is a culinary exploration of Abu Dhabi’s diverse flavors and cultural traditions. Written by Hanan Sayed Worrell, the book presents a collection of recipes and stories from expatriates and locals alike, showcasing the fusion of global influences and Emirati cuisine. It celebrates the cultural diversity and community bonds formed through the sharing of food and traditions in Abu Dhabi.
