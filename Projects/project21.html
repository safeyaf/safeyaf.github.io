Publication: Panoramic Beirut

Publication Design at PlanBEY, an Artesian Publishing House based in Beirut, Lebanon. The publication carries the title “Panoramic Beirut: 1870 -2017”, and displays the transformation of Beirut throughout the decades. The book is structured in from of a folder with devisions according to the year. Panoramic leporellos guided by texts of Antoine Atallah, a Lebanese urbanest living in Paris, France. The publication was published in Beirut in February 2018. Limited edition of 500 copies.

Commissioned work for: PlanBEY, Artesian Publishing House
Design by: Safeya Fawzy & Karma Tohme
Author: Antoine Atallah
PlanBEY, Beirut, Lebanon | 2017
