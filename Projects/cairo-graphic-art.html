Cairo Graphic Art
      / Exhibition

Design and branding for the Fourth National Graphic Art Exhibition included the creation of a poster and an exhibition catalog. Held at the Palace of Art within the Cairo Opera House, the event showcased a diverse range of printmaking techniques, including linoleum and woodblock printing, etching and silkscreen printing.

Featuring works by prominent Egyptian artists and emerging talents, the exhibition celebrated the diversity and creativity of graphic art in Egypt. The poster, a key element of the event's visual identity, was selected as one of the 100 Best Arabic Posters of 2016.
