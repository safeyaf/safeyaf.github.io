Dainas:
A letterpress book

Dainas are short Latvian poems. They are very mystical and include many natural elements, and earth driven metaphors. This book displays nine letter-pressed Latvian poems, their German translation and interpretation. Accompanied by hand printed nature elements guiding through the poems. A hand bound and hand printed book, fitting the natural feel of the poems.

Supervised by Prof. Juergen Huber
Team Members: Anne Ludwig, Isable Stolze
HTW University of Applied Sciences Berlin | 2013
