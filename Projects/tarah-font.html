     Tarah Font

The typeface “Tarah” is an experimental Arabic font design inspired by the geometric precision of the Kufi script, reimagined with a modern edge. This unique typeface, notable for its bold shapes and clean lines, was brought to life in a series of handprinted silkscreen postcards.

Each postcard showcases the phrase كل تأخيرة فيها خيرة (translating to “In every delay, lies something good”), an Arabic saying that conveys the wisdom of patience and trusting in the timing of events beyond one’s control. The message, paired with Tarah’s striking geometric forms, creates a visually resonant piece of art that emphasizes both cultural heritage and contemporary design.

Silkscreen. Limited edition of 50 | 2021
