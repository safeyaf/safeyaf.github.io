Sign System: Elimination

Jerusalem is an Arabic and Palestinian city. It is considered sacred by the three-Abrahamic religions: Islam, Christianity, and Judaism. Jerusalem is divided into four quarters; the Armenian, Christian, Jewish and Muslim quarter. However because of the current Israeli occupation, the city of Jerusalem is under the threat of losing it's genuine identity; history is being twisted in order to transform it into a Jewish city. This project is an attempt to show the cities diminishing identity.

Supervised by Ghalia Elsrakbi
Team members: Yosra Gamal, Sabah Khaled
Project photography by Ramy Radwan
