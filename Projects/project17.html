    Temporary
        Exhibition

The exhibition, titled “Temporary | Metamorphosis of a City: Beirut 1870 - 2052”, showcased the transformative journey of Beirut across the decades. The exhibition design focused on highlighting the city's evolution, from its Ottoman Arabesque architectural style in 1870, to its status as a French colony in 1952, through to the destruction it faced following the Lebanese Civil War in 1995 and culminating in the towering structures of modern-day Beirut. A visual identity was developed for the exhibition, encompassing a brochure, posters and display materials.



Exhibition curators:
Antione Sfeir and Karma Tohme

Graphic design:
Safeya Fawzy

Commissioned work for:
PlanBEY, Artesian Publishing House Beirut, Lebanon | 2017
