Stomped: Photobook

Stomped is a photography series that documents metal shapes that melted into the asphalt ground of Cairo's streets. Lost metal key-chains, Cola cans and personal belongings become fossils in the street. The metal melts because of the massive heat and crossing car tires flatten the elements, like a press. The book, is in a from of a Leporello, resembling a street you walk along.

Supervised by Hannes Wanderer
HTW University of Applied Sciences Berlin | 2013
