           11 /
     Future
        / Record
           

Future Record is an experimental publication born from a six-day visual research project conducted in 2021. Through material and form explorations, the project gave rise to a range of imagined future artifacts; particles that appear to shimmer between the “no-more“ and the “not-yet.“ Capturing traces of both possible futures and pasts, the project resulted in a collaborative publication that presents a multi-perspective look at these speculative worlds.

My contribution involved experimenting with wax and photography, creating visual compositions that document these fragile, ephemeral moments suspended in time.

Publication participants:
Andrea Kahlke, Angelina Simon, Anja Germanova, Ann-Sophie Kettlitz, Lina Jeppener, Lisa Semrau, Louise Preuss, Merle Koenig, Pauline Heppeler, Safeya Fawzy, Stefanie Henningsen, Svea Schroeder

Concept: Jian Haake | Wax workshop: Nele Kieseritzky

Muthesius University | 2021
