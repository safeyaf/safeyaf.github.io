     Fanzine
        / Egyptian
  Cinema Titles

This fanzine is a vibrant booklet dedicated to the world of Egyptian cinema, an art form celebrated across the Arabic-speaking world, where films and dialogues are widely known and recited. It explores the distinct and playful typography used in classic Egyptian film titles, capturing the unique, experimental letterings that shaped the visual identity of this cinematic era. The pages are riso-printed in two bold colors.

The fanzine not only showcases the funky, iconic type but also brings to life the texture and artistry that embody Egypt’s rich film heritage. It’s a collector’s piece for cinephiles and design enthusiasts alike, blending nostalgia with typographic fascination.

Fanzine. Muthesius University | 2023
